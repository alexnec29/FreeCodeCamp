Topic 1

Social media has become an integral part of modern life, offering connectivity and information at our fingertips. However, its pervasive presence can also have significant implications for mental health.

Studies have shown that excessive use of social media platforms can contribute to feelings of anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem, as individuals compare their lives to carefully curated representations of others.
